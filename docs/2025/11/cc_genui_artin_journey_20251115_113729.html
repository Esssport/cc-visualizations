Tactile & Vestibular

  • Skin is richest sensory organ: 5 million touch receptors sending constant data
  • Vestibular system (inner ear balance): Detecting head position and movement
  • Why babies love being held: Touch releases oxytocin, reduces cortisol stress hormones
